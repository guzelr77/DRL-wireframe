About DRL

DRL is a clinical research organization providing pharma and biotech with human biospecimens, primary cell lines, 3D tumor models, and GLP-aligned preclinical services. Founded in 2016, we operate laboratories in the US and Europe. By controlling our own facilities, we maintain full quality oversight, competitive pricing, and faster turnaround.
